# Omara Payments Brand Guidelines

## Logo Usage

The Omara Payments logo is the primary visual identifier for the brand. It represents modern financial technology, fluidity, and growth.

### Logo Variations

1.  **Full Logo**: Used for navigation bars, official documents, and large marketing materials.
    *   **Composition**: Icon + "Omara" text.
    *   **Usage**: Primary brand representation.
2.  **Icon Only**: Used for favicons, mobile app icons, compact headers, and avatars.
    *   **Composition**: The "P" shaped fluid mark.
    *   **Usage**: When space is limited or brand is already established.

### Logo Colors

*   **Primary Blue**: `#2563eb` (Tailwind `blue-600`) - Trust, stability.
*   **Cyan Accent**: `#06b6d4` (Tailwind `cyan-500`) - Innovation, technology.
*   **White**: `#ffffff` - Used on dark backgrounds.
*   **Black/Dark Gray**: `#0f172a` (Tailwind `slate-900`) - Used on light backgrounds.

### Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equivalent to 50% of the icon's height to ensure legibility and impact.

### Do's and Don'ts

*   **Do**: Use the official SVG or high-resolution PNG files.
*   **Do**: Scale the logo proportionally.
*   **Don't**: Stretch, skew, or distort the logo.
*   **Don't**: Change the colors of the logo elements.
*   **Don't**: Place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast.

### Responsive Sizing

*   **Desktop Navbar**: Height `32px` - `40px`
*   **Mobile Navbar**: Height `28px` - `32px`
*   **Footer**: Height `32px`
*   **Favicon**: `32x32`, `16x16`, `192x192` (App Icon)

## Typography

*   **Primary Font**: Inter (Sans-serif) - Clean, modern, highly legible.
*   **Headings**: Bold, often used with gradients for emphasis.

## Assets Location

All logo assets are stored in `src/assets/logo/` or referenced directly via CDN for optimization.
